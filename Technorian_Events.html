CALL OF DUTY

About Event

Men in Action is a thrilling event inspired by James Bond and John Wick. All players will be divided into two teams of 25 players each. The aim of the game is to complete the assigned missions in the given time. Each mission accomplished would earn some points. The last man standing would be the winner of the Challenge.

Rules:

1. All the missions must be completed within the campus.
2. Inappropriate/Abusive language is prohibited.
3. No missions can be performed on Continental grounds.
4. It is mandatory to complete all the tasks.
5. After every successfully completed task upload a photo proof.
6. Any damage or loss of property, lent by the event organizers would be held against the player responsible.

Awards:

1st prize: 1000/-
2nd prize: 500/-

Entry Fee:

Solo - 50/-

Faculty Coordinator : Prof N Mungale
Student Coordinator : Divyanshu Sindpure (Mobile No : 9765097940)
